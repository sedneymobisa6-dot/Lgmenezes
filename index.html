The Founder

Luis G. Menezes

Luis G. Menezes is the architect of one of Western Kenya's most enduring legal institutions. A graduate of the Kenya School of Law (1969), he was among the pioneering generation of Kenyan advocates who built the country's legal infrastructure in the post-independence era.

Before founding the firm, Mr. Menezes served as a lecturer at the University of Nairobi — a formative experience that shaped his conviction that the law must be accessible, understood, and applied with rigour. On 11th May 1970, he opened L.G. Menezes & Co Advocates on Miriu Street in Kisumu, beginning a practice that would grow to define legal service in the Lake Victoria region.

More than five decades later, the firm he founded continues to carry forward his founding principles: depth of knowledge, personal service, and an unwavering commitment to the client's best interest.

Our Journey

Five Decades, One Commitment

A firm is not measured by its age, but by what it has stood for across the years.

1970

The Founding

Luis G. Menezes opens L.G. Menezes & Co Advocates on Miriu Street, Kisumu on 11th May 1970. The firm begins with a focus on civil litigation and property transactions in the Lake Victoria region.

1975

Banking & Commercial Practice Established

As Kisumu's commercial sector grows, the firm expands to serve banking institutions and corporate clients, establishing a dedicated commercial law practice.

1985

L.G.M. House — A Permanent Home

The firm moves into L.G.M. House on Miriu Street, establishing a permanent, purpose-built home that remains the head office to this day.

1995

Nairobi Association

A formal association with a Nairobi office is established — Menezes & Partners Advocates — extending the firm's reach to the national capital and offering clients seamless pan-Kenya legal services.

2005

Mombasa Office Opens

Expanding to the coast, the firm opens its Mombasa presence, completing a national footprint across Kisumu, Nairobi and Mombasa.

2010

New Constitutional Era

Following Kenya's promulgation of the 2010 Constitution, the firm deepens its constitutional law and devolution practice, advising clients on the evolving legal landscape.

2020

Golden Jubilee — 50 Years of Excellence

The firm marks its 50th anniversary — a milestone that few Kenyan law firms reach — with a renewed commitment to the next generation of legal service.

Today

Continuing the Legacy

Over 55 years on, L.G. Menezes & Co Advocates continues to serve clients across Kenya and beyond, combining the depth of decades with the responsiveness demanded by a changing world.
